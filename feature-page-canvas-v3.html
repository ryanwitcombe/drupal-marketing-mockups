Capabilities

Features that make Drupal Canvas the builder your team wants

Create and publish. Without bottlenecks.

Your marketing team launches pages — not tickets

Canvas puts page creation in the hands of the people who know the content best. No developer queue, no deployment pipeline, no waiting.

Drag-and-drop visual editor: Build pages by arranging sections, columns, and components visually.
Pre-built component library: Heroes, grids, CTAs, testimonials — production-ready from day one.
Real-time preview: See your page on desktop, tablet, and mobile before publishing.
AI-assisted content: Generate draft copy, suggest layouts, optimise — all inside the editor.
Instant publishing: Hit publish. Your page is live. No build step, no deployment.
[ Canvas editor screenshot ]
All the control. None of the chaos.

Enterprise governance without enterprise friction

Developers define the rules. Marketers work within them. Content stays on-brand and on-process without slowing anyone down.

Role-based permissions: Control who can edit what, down to the component level.
Approval workflows: Route content through review before it goes live.
Content staging: Prepare and preview entire page updates before publishing.
Design guardrails: Developers define component rules — marketers can't break the design system.
[ Permissions / workflow screenshot ]
Built on Drupal. Built to last.

The architecture developers trust

Canvas isn't a bolt-on. It runs on Drupal's entity and field system — the same foundation that powers the world's most demanding digital properties.

Entity-based architecture: Every component is a Drupal entity. Queryable, fieldable, translatable.
API-first: Headless-ready. Serve content to any front-end, any channel.
Multilingual: Built-in translation workflows for every piece of content.
Open source: No vendor lock-in. 10,000+ security contributors. Your data, your platform.
