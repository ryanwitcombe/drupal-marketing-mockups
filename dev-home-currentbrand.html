New to Drupal?

You can have a working Drupal site in 15 minutes.

If you've heard Drupal is hard to get started with, that's not the project we're building today. Here are the three fastest paths from zero to a real Drupal site you can click around in — pick the one that suits you.

⚡ ~15 minutes · No install

Try Drupal in your browser

Spin up a real, working Drupal CMS site in the cloud. No Composer, no Docker, no database setup. Click around, build pages with Canvas, install modules, see how it all fits together.

  • Pre-installed Drupal CMS with sample content
  • Drupal Canvas page builder ready to go
  • Free trial — no credit card required
Launch a sandbox ~30 min · Local install

Install Drupal locally

The traditional way. Set up Drupal on your own machine with DDEV — the recommended local development environment.

  • Step-by-step quick-start guide
  • Works on macOS, Linux, Windows
  • Full developer environment

Contribution

Drupal is built by the people who use it.

There's no single company behind Drupal — there's a global community of developers, designers, marketers, agencies, and end users contributing time, code, and expertise. Joining is the same on day one as on year ten: pick a path, ship a contribution, get credited.

Contributing to Drupal isn't just code. It's docs, design, mentoring, organising events, translating — every kind of contribution counts.
— Drupal Community Working Group
01
