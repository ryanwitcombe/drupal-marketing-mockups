Frequently asked questions

What marketers want to know

Yes. Drupal Canvas is a visual drag-and-drop page builder designed for marketers. You can create pages from pre-built components, preview them across devices, and publish instantly — no code, no deployment pipeline, and no developer ticket required. Your developers define the component library and design rules; you use them freely within those guardrails.

Drupal CMS offers enterprise-grade security, built-in multilingual support, and structured content architecture that WordPress requires plugins to achieve. With Drupal Canvas, marketing teams get a visual editing experience comparable to WordPress page builders — but backed by an architecture that scales to complex, multi-site, multi-language environments without security trade-offs.

Drupal CMS includes AI-powered content generation, layout suggestions, SEO optimisation assistance, and one-click translation — all built into the editor. The AI integration is open and vendor-neutral, so you're not locked into a single AI provider. Your organisation retains full control over how AI is used and what data it accesses.

Drupal CMS is built on the same enterprise architecture trusted by governments, universities, and global media organisations for over 20 years. It includes role-based permissions, approval workflows, content staging, API-first architecture, and a security track record backed by over 10,000 contributors. Your IT team defines the rules; your marketing team works within them.

Yes. Drupal CMS is fully open source, which means no licensing fees, no vendor lock-in, and complete ownership of your data and platform. You can host it anywhere, customise it fully, and switch providers at any time. Open source also means a global community of contributors continuously improving the platform's security, performance, and capabilities.
